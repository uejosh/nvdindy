DENTAL CARE FROM PEOPLE WHO CARE.

Northview Dental is a family practice with an upbeat, friendly environment. Our dental team is knowledgeable and highly trained, and our dentist and staff are gentle and can put any patient at ease.

Meet the Doctor

Dr. Brody Goodwine

Dr. Brody Goodwine is a long-time resident of Indianapolis, and he has a tireless drive to deliver cutting-edge dental care and comfortable experiences. He is an Omicron Kappa Upsilon dental honorary inductee, reserved for the top clinicians within the top 5% of dentists, and graduated with the highest honors from Indiana University School of Dentistry.

While “Dr. Brody” is invested in the finest technology available, he understands that building trust and treating people with integrity is what defines exceptional care. Come visit Dr. Goodwine at Northview Dental and let us show you the quality of care we will provide!

Dr. Mathew Kavanaugh

Dr Kavanaugh grew up in Ligonier, IN and attended West Noble High School. He received a Bachelor of Science degree in Chemistry at Indiana University in Bloomington and received his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from Indiana University School of Dentistry in 2005. Dr Kavanaugh practiced in Ft Wayne from 2005-2007 and then purchased a practice in Indianapolis. He practiced at Kavanaugh Dentistry for 10 years before joining his office with Northview Dental of Indianapolis in 2018. He loves the fantastic team here at Northview and enjoys using the newest technology to make patient care easier, more efficient, and more comfortable. He likes getting to know patients and is passionate about giving them the best dental care possible. Dr Kavanaugh focuses on preventive care and treatment planning for predictable outcomes. In his spare time, he enjoys IU sports, playing golf, and Golden Tee. He loves spending time with his fiancee, Chelsea, and his dog, Bones. Some of their favorite things are trips to Lake Wawasee, spending time on the patio, and walks around their South Broad Ripple neighborhood.
