DENTAL SERVICES & TECHNOLOGIES

Northview Dental is thrilled to provide dental services to patients of all ages. Each service is delivered with the highest standard of quality and done so by a team that truly cares about you.

General & Cosmetic Dentistry for the whole family.

Drs. Brody Goodwine & Mathew Kavanaugh from Northview Dental have provided personalized dental care to thousands of families. Our patients come from near and far to experience the warm, individualized care we provide. We pride ourselves in utilizing the most modern equipment and methods available.

In order to provide nothing but the best to our patients, our office uses state-of-the-art technologies in all our procedures. These new technologies allow us to detect dental concerns at early stages, treat your dental issues with precision, and provide stunning and customized restorations.
