Dental Savings Plan

At Northview Dental, we understand that not everyone has access to traditional dental insurance, and some people chose not to have dental insurance. With this in mind, we are proud to introduce our Dental Savings Plan to provide our patients affordable access to the quality care they need.

Our specially designed savings plan provides our patients and their families with significant advantages and choices in dental care. It provides a reliable, convenient alternative financial solution to help you achieve your goals.

REASONS TO JOIN:

  • No Annual Maximum
  • No Pre-Authorizations
  • No Limitations
  • No Waiting Period
  • No Deductible
  • No Claims

SERVICES IN PLAN:

  • Diagnostics
  • Preventive Care
  • Restoration Dentistry
  • Periodontal Care
  • Surgery
  • Implants
  • Orthodontics
  • And more!

Routine cleanings, exams and x-rays are all included in the cost of membership. Plus, we offer member-only discounts and benefits on additional services. You can purchase a single membership or a membership for your family, and all services are valid for one year from the date you enroll. If you have any additional questions about our savings plan, please don’t hesitate to call.

PLAN OPTIONS:

  • Single - $500
  • Dual - $925
  • Family of 3 - $1,3500
  • Family of 4 - $1,500
  • Additional Member - $450

PLAN BENEFITS:

  • Periodic Exams 2/yr
  • Cavity Detection X-rays 1/yr
  • Adult/Child Prophy 2/yr **Periodontal Maintenance Excluded
  • Fluoride Treatment 1/yr
  • Head and Neck Exam 1/yr
  • Oral Cancer Screening 1/yr

Routine dentistry plays a crucial role in maintaining optimal oral health and overall well-being. By maintaining regular dental check-ups, we can catch potential dental issues early on, prevent the progression of dental problems, and preserve natural teeth for a lifetime. Additionally, routine dentistry helps maintain a beautiful smile, boosts confidence, and promotes better overall health. Don’t delay! Sign up, save and take the first step towards a healthier, happier you.
